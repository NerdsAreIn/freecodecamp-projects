Buffy Anne Summers

She saved the world. A lot.

A Timeline of Buffy's Life

  • 1981: Born on January 19th.
  • 1996: Chosen as Slayer and assigned Merrick Jamison-Smythe as Watcher.
  • 1997: Moves with her mother to Sunnydale, California, which, as destiny would have it, was situated on a Hellmouth: a centre of supernatural activity.
  • 1997: Faces off against a legendary and ancient vampire, the Master, and dies - in accordance with prophecy - by drowning. She is revived and vanquishes the Master.
  • 1999: Successfully passes the Cruciamentum - a gruelling test of skill and initiative set for the slayer on her eighteenth birthday by the Watchers' Council.
  • 1999: Parts ways with the Watchers' Council, graduates from Sunnydale High School and matriculates at UC Sunnydale.
  • 2001: Sacrifices herself to prevent the coming of Hell on Earth - sparing the life of her younger "sister", Dawn, who was actually the human embodiment of a mystical ball of energy, the Key.
  • 2001: Buffy's friends bring her back to life for the second time and she resumes her duties with a heavy heart.
  • 2003: Confronted with the First Evil, Buffy has the idea to empower all of the potential slayers across the world via her friend Willow's magic - creating an army to battle the First's warrior vampires. Sunnydale is destroyed in the final battle, while Buffy emerges victorious.
